Hey Buddy! Check out my Projects!

One of my most favourite past times is working on my projects. It allows me to combine logic to creativity which I find is where the world's best innovation profoundly derives from. Some of these projects were created while in Hackathons while others were created by myself alone in my pajamas in the dark.

Grubit App

Developed an app that helps indecisive users decide what to eat as well as giving them the opportunity to explore the world of cuisine in the palm of their hands - integrated Yelps and Google Maps API.

MYO Ninja

Using the Thalmic Labs Platform and coding in C++, created a multiplayer reaction-based game using the Myo Armband. This was my first Myo product that allowed me to use forearm muscle detections to create a quick and physical game!

Favors App

The Favours App allows users to post tasks they would like to be done as well as offer their services in return - essentially taking payments with cash out of the picture and returning a favour after theirs have been completed.

BajGuide Robot

The robot is created to fulfill the role as a guide-dog and a tour guide by recognizing location, speech, QR codes, and other forms of commands. Using only recycled tech, the BajGuide idea is to revolutionize the human-technology relationship.

LDAP

Developed a daily input system for the Lung Diagnostic Assessment Program that allows for hospital staff professionals to efficiently and effectively store patient's diagnosis and test results - currently being used actively today.
