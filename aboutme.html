Get to know me ...Personally ;)

So I have a ton hobbies and interests and sometimes, I get pretty intensed. I'm not a pro or anything but I love to pick up new sports and activities!

"A moment of Pain is worth a life-time of glory."
Creative Interests

Being able to imagine, sketch, and create has been one of my life's greatest obsessions. Whether it is in a kitchen cooking my famous cheese Chow Mein or redesigning my whole house, I have an odd sense of appreciation for beautiful design and innovative craft.

I Play

A ton of sports. From badminton to kick boxing, getting physical has been a big part of my daily life. I enjoy the challenges of mind over matter and strive everyday to not only better myself as an athlete but as a person.

I travel

Everywhere I possibly can. Seeing the world in every angle and experiencing new memories is truly an essence of life. When I retire, my dream is to go backpacking around the globe. Fingers crossed!

I Laugh

At basically everything. I enjoy telling jokes and when someone laughs so hard they lose balance, I consider that an accomplishment. I believe that everyone in the world deserves at least 100 laughs each day and happiness is essential.

Yes, Hackathons

Attending 36 hour hackathons with no sleep have oddly became a passion of mine. When a group of motivated people are crammed in a room in front of computers, interesting things happen.

I Eat

With love. I'm a huge foodie that not only loves to cook but to eat good food. Maybe that's why I'm a little too heavy... But that's okay! Eat Happy Live Well!

My Friends

Are the absolute best. I love surrounding myself with great people that not only inspire me but teach me new things. I believe every single person in this world has their own story and I am proud to say I am apart of theirs as much as they are a part of mine.

Competitive Athlete - Sorta

I have won 4 city championships and competed in the 2013 OFSAA Badminton Championships. As for kung fu, I spent 16 years training and teaching for Hong Luck Kung Fu Club - Canada's oldest Kung Fu club and held the title Junior Shaolin Kung Fu Champion. Don't get the wrong idea though...I'm pretty overweight now :(

I can't swim but I like sailing.
